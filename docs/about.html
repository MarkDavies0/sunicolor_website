SUNIcolor

How do I use it?

Colors are shown in groups of two, the left showing what the color looks like in light mode and the right in dark mode.
Note: colors with an alpha component are displayed over a white background.
Simply click on a color swatch to copy the corresponding UIColor initializer. Use the buttons below the search bar to select the language, Swift or Objective-C.

Where did these colors come from?

The colors are from internal iOS frameworks such as ChatKit, SiriUI, and FitnessUI. These color constants are defined by Apple but are private and inaccessible to developers. In addition SUNIcolor also includes all public UIColor constants.
Learn more about private frameworks (blog post by Fernando Bunn).

How did you find them?
